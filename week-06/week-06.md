# Week 6 - Retroactive Sprint Plan

This week we focused on creating a retrospective action plan for the sprint week. 
We reflected on several areas including what went well, what we learned during the sprint, 
the challenges we faced, and the things we felt were missing during the process.

Through discussion we were able to identify both our strengths and weaknesses as a team,
and recognising these areas helped us understand how we can improve our workflow and collaboration in the next
sprint week.

Based on the reflections we created an action plan to improve several aspects of our development 
process moving forward. These included increasing test coverage, improving team communication, clarifying task requirements, 
integrating code more frequently, and maintaining better documentation. For each improvement area 
we also defined success measures so that we can track our progress in upcoming sprints.

This retrospective allowed us to reflect on our experiences during the sprint and 
identify steps to improve both our productivity and teamwork moving forward.
