# Day 5 - Sprint Week 2

Today was mostly spent finalising changes, merging code to the dev-racebot branch, and preparing for the final live demo to John from IBM. The vast majority of the bugs
were fixed, including some minor graphing errors we got when the car completed a lap.

Our race-car got an overall lap time of 2 minutes 30 seconds, which was not our fastest lap, however as mentioned there was a degree of unreliability and consitency from
the car when it was pushed to its full potential. So we decided to be safe rather than sorry, and we capped our cars speed and other variables to lower the overall lap time,
in exchange for a more reliable, almost certain 2 minute 30 second time.
