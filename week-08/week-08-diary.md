# Week 8 - Retroactive Plan

This week I focused on finishing and preparing to submit the assignment. I spent a lot of time working on the retrospective action plan, making sure it clearly showed 
how our team performed throughout the module.

I looked back at the work we did in earlier sprints and made sure our reflections on what went well, what didn’t, and what could be improved were clear and backed 
up with examples. I included things like communication, testing, and integration, and explained how these improved over time. I also made sure the action points were 
realistic and had clear outcomes.

As well as the retrospective, I worked on improving the overall assignment. I checked the structure, made things clearer, and made sure everything was consistent and 
easy to follow. I also looked back at feedback from previous weeks to make sure we had fixed any issues.

Overall, this week was about bringing everything together and presenting our work in a clear and organised way. It also gave me a chance to reflect on the progress 
we made and see how our way of working improved over time.
